# 💰 Price Estimation — UHAS-Basic School Management System

> **Date:** May 29, 2026
> **Currency:** Ghana Cedis (GH₵)

---

## 1. System Scope Summary

| Component | Details |
|---|---|
| **Frontend (Web)** | Vite + React SPA, 22 page modules, premium glassmorphism UI, role-based dashboards |
| **Backend API** | Express.js, 24 REST route files, 24 controllers (~250KB of business logic) |
| **Mobile App** | React Native / Expo companion app |
| **Database** | Supabase (PostgreSQL) with RLS, 7+ relational tables, seeder scripts |
| **Auth & Security** | JWT authentication, role-based access (Admin, Teacher, Student, Parent) |

### Key Features Included

- Attendance tracking with punctuality intelligence
- Timetable scheduling with collision detection
- Marks entry & grading (weighted assessments)
- Academic report card generation
- Fee management & payment tracking
- Financial reports (income, expenses)
- Staff management
- Assignments module
- Events & calendar
- Announcements
- Student promotion engine
- Parent portal with sibling linking
- Activity/audit logs
- System settings & configuration
- 4 role-based dashboards (Admin, Teacher, Student, Parent)

## 7. Notes & Assumptions

1. Estimates are based on **Ghana-based developer rates** of GH₵ 2,500 – GH₵ 6,000/month for mid-level developers.
2. The **mobile app** significantly adds to cost. Without it, subtract approximately GH₵ 10,000 – 15,000 from totals.
3. Prices reflect the **current state** of the system as built — a production-ready, feature-rich platform with premium UI/UX.
4. Exchange rate used: **GH₵ 11.72 = $1 USD** (as of May 2026). Rates fluctuate; USD equivalents are approximate.
5. Prices do not include costs for third-party integrations (e.g., payment gateways like MTN MoMo, Vodafone Cash) which would add GH₵ 5,000 – 10,000.
